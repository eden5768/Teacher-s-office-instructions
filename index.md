**How to Find Professor Cooke’s Office from PICHO 246**

**Introduction**

Finding a teachers office as a new student can be intimidating. With a
campus this big it makes it hard to get from point A to point B. In this
instruction, it will show how to find a teachers office and how to get
there from the classroom. In this example, it will show how to get to
Professor Cooke’s office from the classroom that she teaches out of.

[<u>Santa Catalina Hall
location</u>](https://goo.gl/maps/QshF7oeVnSQe1MeX8)

**Instructions:**

**Step 1: Finding the address**

1.1 - Login to your Canvas

1.2 - Select TWC 401/501 as seen in image #.

<img src="photo/image22.png" style="width:2.20313in;height:1.53228in" />

1.3 - Click on the syllabus icon on the left hand side of the screen.

<img src="photo/image26.png" style="width:1.56771in;height:1.64697in" />

1.4 - Click on the link next to the text that says “Here is the course”.

<img src="photo/image27.png" style="width:6.5in;height:1.30556in" />

1.5 - Find the section that says office at the top of the screen.

<img src="photo/image24.png" style="width:6.5in;height:1.59722in" />

1.6 - Write down the building and room number on your phone or a piece
of paper.

1.7 - Go to this link.
[<u>Map</u>](https://www.asu.edu/map/interactive/)

1.8 - Click on the book icon on the left side of the screen.

<img src="photo/image18.png" style="width:0.71354in;height:2.61458in" />

1.9 - Click on Polytechnic campus.

<img src="photo/image10.png" style="width:1.91146in;height:2.96241in" />

1.10 - Find the building your classroom is in.

<img src="photo/image18.png" style="width:2.76563in;height:2.1569in" />

1.11 - Write down the building's name.

1.12 - Find the building your teacher’s office is in.

<img src="photo/image6.png" style="width:2.657in;height:2.63021in" />

1.13 - Write down the building's name.

**Step 2: Going to the Address**

Note: The starting point is considered as PICHO 246.

<img src="photo/image9.jpg" style="width:1.70703in;height:2.27604in" />

2.1 - Exit classroom ‘PICHO 246’.

<img src="photo/image23.jpg" style="width:1.54688in;height:2.06543in" />

2.2 - Take the left exit.

<img src="photo/image20.jpg" style="width:1.85547in;height:2.47396in" />

2.3 - Cross the bridge.

<img src="photo/image5.jpg" style="width:1.83701in;height:2.44935in" />

2.4 - Take the right and walk straight.

<img src="photo/image21.jpg" style="width:1.77604in;height:2.36806in" />

2.5 - Take the first left and go downstairs.

<img src="photo/image19.jpg" style="width:1.66146in;height:2.22266in" />

2.6 - Walk straight and exit the building. You are now on “E Desert
Mall”.

<img src="photo/image16.jpg" style="width:1.32554in;height:1.76494in" /><img src="photo/image15.jpg" style="width:1.31771in;height:1.76573in" />

2.7 - Take a left on “E Desert Mall” and keep walking straight. You will
pass the Santan Hall.

<img src="photo/image4.jpg" style="width:1.29297in;height:1.72773in" />

2.8 - Keep walking straight.

<img src="photo/image11.jpg" style="width:1.53407in;height:2.05065in" /><img src="photo/image12.jpg" style="width:1.53646in;height:2.07488in" />

2.9 - Take a left and enter Santa Catalina Hall.

<img src="photo/image13.jpg" style="width:2.21354in;height:1.6538in" />

2.10 - Take the elevator on your right.

<img src="photo/image7.jpg" style="width:1.78129in;height:2.36692in" />

2.11 - Press on “2” to go to the second floor of the building.

<img src="photo/image14.jpg" style="width:1.5803in;height:2.12644in" />

2.12 - Exit the elevator.

<img src="photo/image2.jpg" style="width:1.52604in;height:2.03889in" />

2.13 - Take the second right.

<img src="photo/image1.jpg" style="width:1.58486in;height:2.11315in" />

2.14 - Enter the 240A-S door.

<img src="photo/image8.jpg" style="width:1.86493in;height:2.48815in" />

2.15 - Take a left.

<img src="photo/image25.jpg" style="width:1.86979in;height:2.49306in" />

2.16 - The third door on your right is Professor Cooke’s office.

**Conclusion**

Finding a teacher’s office can be a simple experience if the following
steps are followed. This set of instructions can be used to find any
teacher's office.
